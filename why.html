Being happy mostly means to notice, to understand that you are happy. You can feel huge happiness and of course I wish it to you. But almost as important to be able to notice and dip into small ones.

They can last several minutes, few seconds or even less. They can consist from visual, tactile sensations, smells, physical feelings all combinations of them. Every moment is unique because everything external can be repeated, but you, your current mood, thoughts, ability to feel and so on - all those unique and exists only 'now'. Be observant and thoughtful. Spy those moments. Be brief but try to find best words to preserve moment.

It's an extra delight to get them out of the jar occasionally. To remember oneself (every single part of your at that moment) and environment, to try to restore even tiny detail. To feel again, not such bright as first time, but adding today's accents. Perhaps every single memory can bring more connected ones... Wipe of the dust and put back into the jar. Till next time.

Your past, your attitude to it determines future you. This can be roots to keep you strong, your wellspring of inspiration, origin of changes and refuge."
